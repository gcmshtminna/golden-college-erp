Department of Public Health

Golden College of Medical Science & Health Technology, Minna

About the Department

The Department of Public Health is dedicated to training professionals who promote, protect and improve the health of communities through education, disease prevention and health promotion strategies. Students gain knowledge in epidemiology, environmental health, community health planning, disease control and public health management.

Career Opportunities

Graduates may work in:

  • Hospitals
  • Primary Healthcare Centres
  • Government Health Agencies
  • NGOs
  • International Health Organizations
  • Research Institutions

Admission Requirements

Five O'Level Credits including English Language, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry and Physics.
